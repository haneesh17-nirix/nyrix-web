Features

Everything your BME department needs

Built specifically for the workflows of Indian medical colleges and hospitals — from complaint logging to final bill settlement.

Service Request Tracking

Log, track, and manage every equipment complaint end-to-end — from initial report to final bill settlement with full timeline history.

Real-time SLA Metrics

RAG (Red-Amber-Green) colour coded SLA dashboard. Monitor vendor delays, processing times, downtime duration, and bill settlement at a glance.

Role-based Access Control

Six built-in roles — BME Admin, BME Staff, Section Clerk, JS Purchase, Principal/ACO — each with precisely scoped permissions. Create custom roles too.

Equipment Registry

Centralised registry of all biomedical equipment with BM numbers, serial numbers, make/model, location, and department mapping.

Multi-tenant SaaS

Designed to serve multiple hospitals independently on a single platform. Each institution's data is fully isolated with their own admin and user base.

Priority & Assignment

Flag requests as Low, Medium, High, or Critical. Assign directly to technicians, add comments, put requests on hold, or deny — all with full audit trail.
