Features

Everything your operations team needs

Built for teams that manage complex, multi-stage service workflows — from the first complaint to the final sign-off.

End-to-end Request Tracking

Log, assign, and monitor every service request from initial complaint to final closure — with a full, timestamped timeline at every stage.

Real-time SLA Metrics

RAG (Red-Amber-Green) coded dashboards surface vendor delays, processing bottlenecks, and overall downtime at a glance — before they breach.

Role-based Access Control

Six built-in roles with precisely scoped permissions — from full admin to read-only executive oversight. Create custom roles as your team evolves.

Asset Registry

A centralised inventory of all tracked assets — with identifiers, make/model, location, and department mapping — always linked to active requests.

Multi-tenant Architecture

Each organisation's data is fully isolated on a shared platform. Onboard new tenants instantly — no separate deployments or infrastructure required.

Priority, Assignment & Audit

Flag requests as Critical, High, Medium, or Low. Assign to team members, add comments, hold or deny — every action logged with a full audit trail.
