Features

Everything your BME operations team needs

Purpose-built for teams managing complex, multi-stage biomedical equipment service workflows — from the first complaint to the final sign-off.

Core
End-to-end Request Tracking

Log, assign, and monitor every service request from initial complaint to final closure — with a full, timestamped activity timeline at every stage. Never lose track of where a request stands.

Monitoring
Real-time SLA & RAG Metrics

RAG (Red-Amber-Green) coded dashboards surface vendor delays, processing bottlenecks, and overall downtime at a glance — giving your team a heads-up before SLAs breach.

Security
Role-based Access Control

Six built-in roles with precisely scoped permissions — from full admin control to read-only executive oversight. Custom roles adapt as your team structure evolves.

Registry
Asset & Equipment Registry

A centralised inventory of all biomedical equipment — with asset IDs, make and model, department mapping, and location — always linked to active service requests.

Architecture
Multi-tenant SaaS

Each institution's data is fully isolated on a shared platform. Onboard new tenants instantly — no separate deployments, no per-tenant infrastructure overhead.

Operations
Priority, Assignment & Audit

Flag requests as Critical, High, Medium, or Low. Assign to technicians, add notes, place on hold, or deny — every action is logged in a tamper-proof audit trail.

Workflow

From complaint to closure

A structured 9-stage pipeline ensures every request moves through a clear, trackable process. Nothing falls through the cracks, no stage is skipped.
