Features

Everything your operations team needs

Purpose-built for teams managing complex, multi-stage service and maintenance workflows — from the first request to the final sign-off, with full accountability at every step.

Core
End-to-end Request Tracking

Log, assign, and monitor every service or maintenance request — from initial complaint to final resolution — with a complete, timestamped activity trail at every stage. Nothing gets lost.

Monitoring
Real-time SLA & RAG Metrics

RAG (Red-Amber-Green) coded dashboards surface delays, bottlenecks, and overdue items at a glance — giving your team the visibility to act before SLA targets are breached.

Security
Role-based Access Control

Multiple built-in roles with precisely scoped permissions — from full departmental admin control to read-only executive oversight. Custom roles adapt to any org structure.

Registry
Asset & Equipment Registry

A centralised inventory of all organisational assets — equipment, facilities, or infrastructure — with asset IDs, make and model, department mapping, and location, always linked to active service requests.

Architecture
Multi-tenant SaaS

Every organisation's data is fully isolated on a shared platform. Onboard a new department or institution in minutes — no separate deployment, no per-tenant infrastructure overhead.

Operations
Priority, Assignment & Audit Trail

Mark requests as Critical, High, Medium, or Low. Assign to staff, add internal notes, place on hold, or deny with a reason — every action is timestamped in a tamper-proof audit log.

Customisable SaaS

Nyrix adapts to your organisation — not the other way around

Every workflow, every role, every approval gate, and every branding element is configurable per organisation. Deploy a standard setup in minutes, then fine-tune it to match exactly how your team works.

Configurable
Custom Workflow Stages

Define the stages that match your actual process. Rename stages, add new ones, or remove steps that don't apply — your pipeline reflects how your team operates, not a generic template.

Governance
Approval Gates & Role Controls

Assign specific roles to each workflow stage. Sensitive changes — like removing a role from a stage — require Principal or management approval before taking effect. Governance is built in.

Branding
Per-organisation Branding

Each organisation sees its own name, department label, and system title inside the app. One platform, many organisations — each with their own identity, never seeing each other's data.

Control
Equipment Approval Workflow

Optionally require management sign-off before any asset in the registry is edited or deactivated — configurable per organisation. Full control over your asset data at every level.

Documentation
File Attachments on Requests

Attach quotations, work orders, sanction letters, invoices, and reports directly to a service request. Secure Azure Blob storage with pre-signed URLs; download any document at any stage.

Analytics
Department & Section Reports

Executive dashboards show request volumes, SLA compliance, and downtime metrics broken down by department and section — giving leadership a clear picture without operational access.

How the customisable SaaS model works
Nyrix is a single hosted platform on Azure. Your organisation gets its own fully isolated tenant — your data never mixes with another organisation's. A platform administrator (Super Admin) onboards organisations and configures the initial setup. Each organisation's Department Administrator then takes over: managing users, configuring workflow stages, setting up the asset registry, and adjusting approval gates. No separate deployments, no software licences, no infrastructure to maintain.
